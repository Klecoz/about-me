Introduction

I'm currently the Kitchen Lead at Resurgence Brewing Company, a new brewery that just opened June 2014. It's almost been a year since I've worked there. I'm also a Certified Beer Server as of April 13th 2015.

I started learning HTML when I was around 11 or 12, and made my first website back when Homestead.com was still around. Since then, I've kinda jumped around and experimented with different subjects. For some reason, I always come back to learning web design/development! I went to SUNY Fredonia for a degree in Video Production but I'm currently in the process of enrolling at SUNY Buffalo for a degree in Independent Studies late 2015.
